## Project Description

- Project by: Phunnipath Theankaew
- Game Genre: RTS 

**All Out War: Shogun** is a real-time strategy game where players act as a warlord aiming to become the Shogun of Japan. Players select one of five clans with unique strengths, manage city upgrades to expand their army, and balance taxation and finances. Poor financial management can lead to debt, while excessive taxation may cause rebellion, making both military strategy and economic control essential to victory.

## Tutorial / Usage
Choose Your Clan: Begin by selecting one of the four primary clans, each with unique specialties:

    Tada: Elite cavalry focus with high base damage (10.5).

    Date: Odachi specialists with high base damage (10.0).

    Nori: Focuses on overwhelming numbers; starts with 180 units.

    Abe: Yari (spear) specialists with balanced stats and cavalry bonuses.

Understand the Right Panel: This is your command center for managing the following:

    Clan Financials: View your treasury, tax income, and upkeep. If your upkeep exceeds your net revenue, you will fall into debt.

    Unit Details: View army stats. You can recruit soldiers from cities; higher city levels allow for more recruitment.

    City Details: Shows current tax rates and "Rage" levels.

    Taxing & Rage: You can set taxes to the maximum, but this increases Rage. If Rage reaches the limit (level 6), you will lose the province to a rebellion.

    Action of Conquest : You will be able to March, Attack, Siege, Ambush, Join Army

- **Marching**: Select your unit on the map and click March in the right panel, then select a destination. Movement speed is dictated by army size; larger armies move slower.

- **Attacking**: Click Attack in the right panel to engage nearby enemies you intercept along routes.

- **Ambush**: Hide in nearby forests by clicking Hide in Forest. This allows you to ambush enemies, forcing them into a fight with a "first-strike" bonus for you.

- **Recruit** / **Upgrade**: Click on your city options in the right panel to either recruit new Soldiers or upgrade the city hall (Village to Citadel).

- **Siege**: Click Siege and select a target enemy city to begin the conquest.

- **Join Army**: Click Join Army to join nearby army within the city.

***You must follow established routes and cannot march past enemy cities or obstacles that do not belong to you.***
